Coldplay

Söker gitarrist

Coldplay are a band from London, England. They have released six albums:
- Parachutes (2000)
- A Rush Of Blood To The Head (2002)
- X&Y (2005)
- Viva La Vida or Death And All His Friends (2008)
- Mylo Xyloto (2011)
- Ghost Stories (2014)
Their seventh album, A Head Full Of Dreams, is out on December 4, 2015.
